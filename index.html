Our Story

Built on Expertise.
Driven by Integrity.

Founded in 2009 by Nason's, we started as a two-man band taking on residential jobs around Walkers Point. Today, we're a team of 24 licensed electricians delivering everything from domestic fault-finding to high-voltage industrial installations.

Our commitment has never changed: do the job right the first time, treat every customer with honesty, and leave every site better than we found it. All work is backed by our 12-month workmanship guarantee.
